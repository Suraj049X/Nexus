Government Polytechnic Solapur

The Government Polytechnic Solapur was Established in the year 1956. Today it houses in its 33 acre campus, a main building, student Hostels, Classrooms for various faculties, Library.
The college offers Diplomas in different Engineering and Technology branches and these courses are affiliated to Maharashtra State Board of Technical Education, Mumbai(Pune Region).
In recognition of various activities , this institute was honoured with prestigious ISTE Narsee Monjee Award for Best Overall Performance during the year 2001.
The institute added another feather to its cap when it was adjudged Best Polytechnic in the State of Maharashtra by the Government of Maharashtra for the year 2001- 2002.

Programs Offered:

  - Mechanical Engineering
  - Civil Engineering
  - Electrical Engineering
  - Electronics and Telecommunication
